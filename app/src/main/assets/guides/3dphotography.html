3D images are generated when each eye sees a slightly different image, resulting in the illusion of depth. With the Orbis camera, you will be able to create your own 3D photographs.

  1. First, press the + at the top right of the gallery page. Select ‘Take’. Take a photo of your object.
  2. Then, holding the camera as steady as possible, move your phone approximately 7 centimetres to the right, and take another photo. Orbis will stitch the photos together and distort them accordingly.
  3. Finally, insert your phone into the Orbis headset to view your image.
Experiment with the distance between shots for increased depth. Wider range will enhance the effect of landscape pictures, while a distance roughly equal to the distance between your eyes is suitable for close-up photos.

In addition to your own images, there are thousands of Virtual Reality photos and videos available on the Internet.
